# {metadata.title}

The Romanian language, like many others, uses definite and indefinite articles
to indicate whether a noun is specific or general.

<br />

The definite article is **required**:

| Rule                                                                                                                               | Example                        |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| When a noun is the subject of a sentence                                                                                           | _Pahar**ul** e pe masă._       |
| After the verb<wbr /> "to like" _("a plăcea")_                                                                                     | _Îmi plăcea ceai**ul**._       |
| When a noun is specified by an adjective or a phrase                                                                               | _Este baiat**ul** frumos._     |
| When a noun is specified by a possessive adjective                                                                                 | _Este cart**ea** mea._         |
| After tot/<wbr />toată/<wbr />toţi/<wbr />toate                                                                                    | _Toate mer**ele** sunt dulci._ |
| When a noun is preceded<wbr /> by the preposition "cu"<wbr /> and refers to an instrument, association, or means of transportation | _Mergem cu elicopter**ul**._   |

<br />

The definite article is **not used**:

| Rule                                                                                                  | Example                            |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| After mult/<wbr />multă/<wbr />mulţi/<wbr />multe and puţin/<wbr />puţină/<wbr />puţini/<wbr />puţine | _Sunt mulți elev**i**._            |
| After numerals                                                                                        | _Sunt 4 barbați în casă._          |
| After the word "each" _("fiecare")_                                                                   | _Fiecare copil primește un cadou._ |
| After cât/<wbr />câtă/<wbr />câţi/<wbr />câte                                                         | _Câte cărț**i**?_                  |
| Most of the times after prepositions                                                                  | _Paharul e pe mas**ă**._           |
| With professions                                                                                      | _Ea este profesoar**ă**._          |
